Privacy Policy

Explain This is a Chrome extension that helps explain selected text using AI services.

What data is processed

  • Selected text: When you use the explain feature, selected text is sent to the configured AI provider to generate a response.
  • API keys: Your API keys are stored locally in chrome.storage.local on your device.
  • Usage stats and history: Simple usage counts and a short history of your recent explanations are stored locally for display inside the extension.

What is not collected

  • We do not collect or store your data on our own servers.
  • We do not track your web browsing history.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties beyond the AI provider you choose.

Where data is stored

All settings, keys, and history are stored locally in your browser. The extension does not operate an external backend or database.

Third-party services

The extension sends selected text securely over HTTPS to the AI provider you configure in settings. Supported providers include:

  • OpenAI API
  • DeepSeek API
  • Gemini API (Google AI Studio)

Please review each provider's policy for how they handle data.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact the repository owner.
