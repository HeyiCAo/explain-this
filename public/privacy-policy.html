Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026

Explain This explains text you explicitly select or enter. This policy covers the free Built-in AI mode, optional Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) mode, and local browser features.

Built-in AI mode

When you request an explanation, the selected text, response language, and explanation style are sent over HTTPS to the Explain This backend. The backend forwards the request to its configured AI provider and streams the answer back.

The application does not persist original selected text or generated explanations in its backend database. Request content is handled transiently in memory.

For quota enforcement and abuse prevention, the backend processes a random installation identifier, a salted hash derived from the request IP address, request counts, and cooldown/reset timestamps. The rate-limit store contains hashes and counters—not selected text, explanations, API keys, or browsing history. This state is deleted 31 days after the bucket's last activity.

Infrastructure providers may process normal delivery metadata under their own service terms. Application logging is configured not to include selected text or response bodies.

Bring Your Own Key mode

BYOK is an optional Advanced feature. Your API key is stored only in chrome.storage.local. BYOK requests go directly from the extension to the provider you select and do not pass through the Explain This backend.

Local browser data

The extension stores preferences, enabled-site settings, the random installation identifier, optional provider keys, and local usage counters in chrome.storage.local. If Save recent explanations is enabled, it also stores up to 10 recent items and 100 cached explanations.

Turning Save recent explanations off clears the local history and cache. Uninstalling the extension removes its local extension storage through Chrome.

Site access and browsing activity

Explain This does not track browsing history. By default, its selection script runs only after you invoke the extension on the active tab. You can optionally allow persistent access to specific domains under Enabled sites and remove that access at any time.

Only text you actively submit is processed. Do not submit passwords, authentication codes, private keys, payment information, or other sensitive data.

Retention and use

  • Selected text and explanations are not persisted by the Explain This backend application.
  • Anonymous quota and abuse counters are deleted 31 days after last activity.
  • Local settings, keys, and optional history remain until cleared or the extension is uninstalled.
  • Operational metadata follows infrastructure-provider settings and terms.

We do not sell personal data or use selected text to train our own models. AI and infrastructure providers may process data under their own terms.

Service providers

Built-in AI uses Cloudflare Workers and Durable Objects for secure relay and anonymous quota enforcement, plus the AI provider configured by Explain This. BYOK uses OpenAI, Google Gemini, or DeepSeek directly, according to your choice. The Built-in AI provider may change without an extension update.

Your choices

You can disable and clear recent explanations, remove enabled-site permissions, clear a BYOK credential, switch AI modes, or uninstall the extension. Anonymous backend quota records contain no account profile or request content.
