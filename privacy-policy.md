# Privacy Policy

Explain This is a Chrome extension that helps explain selected text using AI services.

## What data is processed
- **Selected text**: When you use the explain feature, the selected text is sent to the configured AI provider (e.g., OpenAI, DeepSeek, or Gemini) to generate a response.
- **API keys**: Your API keys are stored locally in `chrome.storage.local` on your device.
- **Usage stats & History**: Simple usage counts and a short history of your recent explanations are stored locally for display in the extension.

## What is not collected
- We do not collect or store your data on our own servers.
- We do not track your web browsing history.
- We do not sell or share your data with any third parties beyond the AI provider you choose.

## Where data is stored
- All settings, keys, and history are stored locally in your browser via `chrome.storage.local`.
- The extension does not operate any external backend or database.

## Third-party services
The extension sends selected text securely over HTTPS to the AI provider you configure in settings. Supported providers include:
- OpenAI API
- DeepSeek API
- Gemini API (Google AI Studio)

Please review each provider’s privacy policy for how they handle data.

## Contact
If you have questions about this policy, contact the repository owner.
